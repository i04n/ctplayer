STEPS TO DEBUGGING FLASH/FLEX ctplayer.swf

1. Copy mm.cfg to C:\Users\cgu1_000\ on Windows or /home/username on Linux

2. Download Flash Player Debugger 20 available here: https://www.adobe.com/support/flashplayer/debug_downloads.html#fp15
and copy flashplayer_20_sa_debug.exe to your desktop. And load the ctplayer.swf from the build folder in the Adobe debugger.

3. Check the logs in: 
Windows: C:\Users\username\AppData\Roaming\Macromedia\Flash Player\Logs\flashlog.txt
Linux: /home/username/.macromedia/Flash_Player/Logs/flashlog.txt

All the logging output will be there.
